Plan Tracker

A beautiful, lightweight, and offline-first progress and routine tracker. It runs instantly on your desktop or mobile browser without requiring any installation, user accounts, or complex server databases.

🎯 What is Momentum?

Momentum is designed to take the mental load off your shoulders. It sits right on your desktop, helping you track long-term project milestones side-by-side with your day-to-day routines. It tells you the exact truth about your schedule with a real-time urgency ticker.

✨ Features

💾 Local-First Storage: All your tasks and progress states are saved automatically to your browser's private localStorage. Close the tab or reboot your laptop—your data stays safe.

⏳ Live Daily Countdown: Shows exactly how many hours and minutes you have left to complete tasks scheduled for "Today," updating dynamically in the background every 30 seconds.

🚦 Smart Sorting Priority: Keeps your board tidy by automatically pushing 🔥 High Priority tasks to the top, and fading completed tasks to the bottom.

🎨 Color-Coded Statuses: Visually track progress instantly with status-select drop-downs that dynamically change color (Slate Grey for Not Started, Amber for In Progress, Emerald Green for Done).

📊 Real-Time Analytics: Interactive metrics at the top keep you updated on total, in-progress, and completed tasks.

📱 Responsive Mobile Design: Designed with a fluid system grid that scales beautifully from an ultra-wide laptop screen to a mobile web browser.

🧹 One-Click Maintenance: Includes an animated modal confirmation to clear out all finished tasks instantly while preserving your long-term goals.

🚀 Quick Start (Running Offline on your Laptop)

You do not need to install anything. You can run Momentum in under a minute:

Download the Code: Copy the code from momentum_tracker.html in this repository.

Create your local file: Create a new text file on your laptop and rename it to momentum_tracker.html (ensure the extension is .html, not .txt).

Run: Double-click the file! It will open instantly in your default web browser.
